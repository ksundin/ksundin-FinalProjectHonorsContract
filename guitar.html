Guitar

Kayli has been playing guitar for over 10 years. She has recieved a guitar performance minor from Arizona State University where she studied under the renowned Frank Koonce. She is proficient on the classical, electric and acoustic guitar, teaching a variety of music styles. Kayli uses the following the curriculm for teaching guitar:

  • Hal Leonard Guitar Method
  • "The Classical Guitar" by Frederick Noad
  • Mel Bay Guitar Method
  • and MORE!
